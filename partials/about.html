About Valet

Valet hopes to make your life as party host, or bored person, slightly more entertaining.

This is a free* service to identify and retrieve music videos based on your Spotify playlists.


Use Valet to share your favorite music on your computer, phone, tablet, or television (with Google Chromecast!) with your friends!

*You may need Spotify premium for the best experience.

Please contact me with any feedback or for the latest updates I have planned. You can find links to Twitter and Github in the navbar!
